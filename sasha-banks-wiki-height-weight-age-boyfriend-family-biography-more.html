Under the ring name “Mercedes KV” she played her debut match on 1st October 2010. On 7th January 2011, she achieved her first victory in a tag match, where she along with Nikki Roxx defeated Alexxis and Mistress Belmont. She has won the Chaotic Women’s Championship on 2nd December 2011.

In 2013, she signed a contract with WWE under the ring name “Sasha Banks”. She has won NXT Women’s Championship in February 2015, by defeating Charlotte Flair in a fatal four-way match. She made her debut on RAW on July 25th, 2016. She has won the RAW Women’s Championship three times.

She got married to Sarath Ton who is a former Indy Wrestler. Currently, he works as a costume designer for WWE. The couple got married on 4th August 2016.
