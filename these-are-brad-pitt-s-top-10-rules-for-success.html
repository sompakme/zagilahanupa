While Brad Pitt was studying journalism in college and wanted to pursue advertising in the future, he became interested in acting during that time. He immediately moved to Los Angeles and began taking on minor roles. Pitt worked as a background actor until receiving his breakthrough with Thelma & Louis, which paved the way for him to take on more interesting roles and venture into various businesses. The actor has been bestowed with various awards, including an Oscar. While movies came to Pitt, the actor took it upon himself to work hard and portray the role to perfection.

The journey from being a background actor to a leading man was full of obstacles, but the actor remained optimistic about getting better results. Through his preparation, generosity, and ability to keep working, Pitt remains a sought-after actor in Hollywood even three decades after his debut. Let’s take a look at Brad Pitt’s top rules for success.

Being Optimistic About The Future

Thelma & Louis is an iconic movie that got Brad Pitt notoriety and the start of his career in Hollywood. The movie didn’t come easily to the actor as he had to beat out other talented young actors like Robert Downey Jr. and George Clooney to become the hitchhiker J.D. The actor only made $6,000 for his role but remained positive that the role would bring in more opportunities.

Chase Your Dreams

The actor made waves with his supporting roles in movies and continued working hard to become a leading man in Hollywood. In 1988, Pitt finally received the role of a lifetime with The Dark Side of the Sun, wherein he portrayed an Adriatic with a skin condition. According to E Online, the role didn’t come easily to him as he beat out 400 prospective candidates to snag the role and earned $1,523 in salary.

Focusing On Mental Health Is Important

Brad Pitt went through a difficult few years after his public reputation had dwindled due to his family issues and legal battles. While he produced movies, Brad Pitt stayed relatively out of the public eye and focused on himself. He made a triumphant return to Hollywood in 2019 as stuntman Cliff Booth in Once Upon A Time In Hollywood, which won him his first acting Oscar.

Help People Around The World

Since the early 2010s, Brad Pitt has made generous donations and helped organizations through the Jolie-Pitt Foundation that he set up with his ex-wife Angelina Jolie. Today, the actor does not hesitate to make donations to various charities. As mentioned by Elle, during the state protests after the death of George Floyd, he matched a $1 million donation to Color of Change with his ex-wife Jennifer Aniston.

Going The Extra Mile For Work

Brad Pitt ensures that he makes each character his own with every role. While trying to memorize the script, he chooses one line that resonates with his character and uses it to bring his emotions onto the screen. Once the movie is wrapped up, he deletes the mental files of his character and moves to the next role.

Take Creative Decisions When Necessary

Brad Pitt and David Fincher have collaborated on multiple projects together, and the partnership began with the crime thriller with 1995 thriller Se7en, wherein Pitt starred alongside Morgan Freeman and Gwyneth Paltrow. Before agreeing to start filming, Pitt made a creative decision about how he wanted the movie’s ending, and the production followed through with his request, as noted by Cheat Sheet.

Training For Each Role

While preparing for his role in David Fincher’s Fight Club, co-starring Edward Norton, Brad Pitt took it upon himself to absorb into his character. For the role of Tyler Durden, Pitt took grappling lessons and practiced taekwondo. The actor also took up soap-making lessons and chipped his front teeth to make a complete transformation.

Learn To Make Choices

While Se7en remains one of the most iconic works of Brad Pitt that still has mystery around it, the actor passed on the role in Apollo 13 to become a part of this project. He chose to give up on historical drama over suspense thrillers. While his mother was disappointed about the role he gave up, the actor does not have any regrets as both movies are equally successful in their genres.

Making The Right Physical Transformations For Each Role

Brad Pitt has always given physical transformations a priority while preparing for any role. The actor has taken on roles from a reverse-aging man to becoming a Greek god, and he never disappointed the audiences with his looks. In Troy, Brad Pitt achieved his greatest physique with muscular workouts, including glute hyperextensions, dumbbell rows, and T-bar rows, as stated by Esquire.

Work Is Worship

After being a part of Hollywood for over thirty years, Brad Pitt is nowhere close to retiring as an actor and producer. He continues to work on interesting projects and delves into every role, be it of a leading man or a supporting actor. Over the last few years, the actor has managed to give some of his career-best performances.

Brad Pitt believes that practice and perfection can take time, but hard work can be an important part of the process. While making creative decisions and taking on roles, the actor wants to challenge himself with unique opportunities. The actor was last seen starring in the 2022 action thriller Bullet Train.
