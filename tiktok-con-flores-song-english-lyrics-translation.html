TikTok: Con Flores Song &amp; English Lyrics Translation

July 08, 2024
blog

What is Con Flores Song? To know everything about the popular TikTok trend and the English translation of the lyrics, keep reading.

TikTok users showed great love for Monsieur Periné’s song Nuestra Canción ft. Vicente García, which many users know as Con Flores song.

The song is from a Bogotá-based musical ensemble. Their music is filled with diversity as they combine Latin and European flavors in it. The members of the group are Catalina García, Nicolás Junca, Santiago Prieto, Adinda Meertins, Jairo Alfonso, Abstin Caviedes, Miguel Guerra, and Darwin Baez.

It is a great example to show how music connects people, even with language differences. Lately, TikTok has become a platform that gives birth to some great as well as weird trends. And It is one of the popular ones among them.

What Is Con Flores Song On TikTok?

Con Flores song of Monsieur Periné is one of the new and popular trends on TikTok.

This new trend goes with #conflores, and thousands of users create content on it. The content usually contains people, indiscriminate of gender, dancing to the tune of the song. The hashtag overall has 49.6 million views, and the top-performing video under it is the one with 6 million views and 1.3 million likes.

Besides being a viral trend on TikTok, it also helps the artist get the exposure needed to grow.

The song also has good reception on streaming platforms like YouTube, where it has almost 2.5 million views and 19 thousand likes. It is the same for Spotify, as it has nearly 30.8 million streams.

TikTok is quite a great platform, especially considering that the song released on 1 September 2016 is trending now, after almost five years.

Con Flores Song English Lyrics Translation

Con Flores Song is a beautiful Spanish song. The song has beautiful lyrics but requires a bit of translation to know.

One portion of the Spanish lyrics of the song, “Con flores te llevaste mi tristeza con colores,” translates to “with flowers you took my sadness, with colors,” which is the best part of the lyrics for most of the people online.

About lyrics, the song describes the love between two people using metaphors related to flowers.

People online say that they don’t understand the song, but it makes them smile. Most of the comments under it say the same thing. They say that they vibe to it, even though they don’t understand.
