In many ways, 30 Rock was the perfect comedy series. It brought together an unlikely cast of characters for truly witty jokes and sharp commentary on politics and culture. It was able to strike a balance between being laugh-out-loud stupid and quite clever. And, unlike so many other great TV series, it called it quits before deteriorating.

We were very saddened to hear of 30 Rock actor Tracy Morgan's recent car accident, which has left him in critical condition in the hospital. As we pray for his speedy recovery, let's celebrate Tracy Jordan, the unpredicatable, loose-cannon of a character Morgan played so well. Here are some of the best Tracy moments from 30 Rock.
