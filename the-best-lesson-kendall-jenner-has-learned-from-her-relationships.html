The Best Lesson Kendall Jenner Has Learned From Her Relationships...

August 21, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner rarely speaks out about her love life, but in a recent behind-the-scenes video for her new Calvin Klein campaign, she opened up about past relationships.

“I think you learn different lessons in every relationship,” US Weekly reports the model saying. “So I think I’ve learned so many different things from so many different relationships.”

The Most Valuable Lesson

So what is the best lesson the model and Keeping Up With The Kardashians star has learned from her headline-making romances?

“A lot of the relationships have taught me more about myself, which is the most valuable,” Jenner explains. “And like, what I want from a person or from a relationship.”

Jenner has been linked to Harry Styles, Jordan Clarkson, Blake Griffin, and A$AP Rocky. She’s recently reconciled with Ben Simmons - they’ve been on and off again since May 2018.

And Why She Keeps Things Under Wraps

Last year, in an interview with Vogue Australia, Jenner explained why she tries to keep her love life private.

“I’m very young and right now I feel like relationships aren’t always super-certain and I don’t want to bring too much attention to something if you don’t really know long term [what it’s going to be],” she told Vogue.

“A relationship is only meant to be between two people, and the second you make it the world’s business is when it starts messing with the two people mentally. Like once everyone is let in, you’re letting all these opinions into your relationships, and I don’t think that’s fair.”

So, What Is The Secret To A Long-Lasting Relationship In 2020?

It’s been said before, but communication is key when it comes to a successful relationship, reports InStyle.

This goes for in person or via social media or other technological channels. If you can find a good balance, that is the most essential of all.
