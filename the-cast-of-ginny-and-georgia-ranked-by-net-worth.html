The Cast Of Ginny And Georgia, Ranked By Net Worth

August 19, 2024
blog

Ginny & Georgia is one of the most popular shows on Netflix right now. Season two was just released on the streaming platform and it seems everyone is binging the dramatic television show. The cast have become highly popular on social media—star Antonia Gentry has amassed nearly 3 million followers on Instagram since the show began in 2021.

Many of the actors and actresses who are members of the cast of Ginny & Georgia can be recognized from other productions. Raymond Ablack, for example, was a star of the Netflix original show The Haunting of Hill House. Felix Mallard has also been in other Netflix productions, as he was a part of the hit television show Locke & Key. Fans always wonder how much celebrities make from working in television and film. Let’s get into the net worths of the cast of Ginny & Georgia.

8 How Much Is Antonia Gentry Worth?

Antonia Gentry has the lead role as Ginny in the hit Netflix original show Ginny & Georgia, and yet she is ranked at the bottom of her castmates when it comes to net worth. This is due to the fact that the last time she reported her net worth, it was 2019. That was before she landed the role of Ginny in Netflix’s Ginny & Georgia.

In 2019, Gentry had a reported net worth of $100,000 to $300,000. Gentry only has five acting credits other than Ginny & Georgia, and two were shorts. Now, as the lead of a Netflix show, Gentry likely has a much larger net worth—especially considering advertisement deals she is making thanks to her nearly 3 million Instagram followers.

7 How Much Is Felix Mallard Worth?

Felix Mallard is an Australian actor who plays Marcus Baker in Ginny & Georgia. His longest running acting gig was with an Australian soap opera. He filmed over 800 episodes for the television show Neighbours, playing the role of Ben Kirk.

After relocating to the United States, Mallard has worked almost exclusively with Netflix. He has been a part of three total productions for the streaming service, including the film All the Bright Places and television shows Locke & Key and Ginny & Georgia. Mallard has a reported net worth of $1 million.

6 How Much Is Nathan Mitchell Worth?

Nathan Mitchell is one of the newest members of the cast of Ginny & Georgia and is worth approximately $1 million. Mitchell plays Zion Miller, ex-boyfriend to Georgia Miller and father to Ginny. Though a younger version of the character was seen throughout the first season of the Netflix show, season 2 is the first time viewers see the adult form of Ginny’s father.

Mitchell has appeared in numerous productions over the years. He stars in Amazon Prime’s The Boys as Black Noir. He also appeared as a guest star in several television shows for the CW network, including Supernatural and iZombie.

5 How Much Is Mason Temple Worth?

Mason Temple, like his co-star and season 1 of Ginny & Georgia love interest Antonia Gentry, is relatively new to the acting scene. Temple plays Hunter Chen in the hit streaming show and was a key member in the Hunter-Ginny-Marcus love triangle.

Before landing the role of Hunter in Netflix’s Ginny & Georgia, Temple only had three acting jobs. Two of them, for iZombie and Six, his characters were unnamed. Temple played Martin in the television show V.C. Andrews’ Landry Family and will appear in the upcoming Boot Camp. Temple has a net worth of $2 million, though some report it as high as $3 million.

4 How Much Is Sara Waisglass Worth?

Sara Waisglass plays fan-favorite Maxine Baker on Ginny & Georgia. Maxine is the best friend to Ginny and twin brother to character Marcus. Though her character may be from the East Coast, Waisglass herself is actually from Canada and always knew she wanted to be an actress.

Waisglass has appeared in numerous productions since first stepping into the entertainment industry in 2007. She played Frankie Hollingsworth in Degrassi: The Next Generation and Degrassi: Next Class. Waisglass has a net worth from her acting jobs of $3 million.

3 How Much Is Scott Porter Worth?

It is no surprise the younger actors and actresses in Ginny & Georgia have lower net worths than the adults. They have not been working in the entertainment industry for as long as someone like Scott Porter.

Porter, who plays Mayor Paul Randolph in the Netflix original show, is best known for his roles in the film Dear John, the successful television show Hart of Dixie, and the show Friday Night Lights. He has also down a lot of voice work for Marvel’s animated shows and video games, though he may be leaving the franchise. He has an estimated net worth of $3 million.

2 How Much Is Raymond Ablack Worth?

Raymond Ablack also has a long list of acting credits to his name. In recent years, Ablack has transferred into playing more adult roles similar to his role as Joe in Ginny & Georgia, but before that he had more teen drama roles.

Ablack can be seen in several episodes of Shadowhunters, and he also played a role in several iterations of the long-running television show Degrassi. Reports of Ablack’s net worth range from $1 million to $5 million, so he likely lands somewhere in the middle around $3 million.

1 How Much Is Brianne Howey Worth?

Brianne Howey plays the iconic and complicated Georgia Miller in Netflix’s Ginny & Georgia. Fans immediately fell in love with Howey and her character, and they even want to know if her sweet Southern accent was real.

Howey began working in the industry before she even graduated from NYU when she landed a guest role in 90210. Since then, she has been working steadily and has appeared in Dollface, The Middle, and Red Scare. She has the highest reported net worth of the Ginny & Georgia cast with $5 million.
