Expecting First Child: Da Brats Wife Shares Pregnancy News

July 04, 2024
blog

Judy, Da Brat’s wife, is 41 years old as of 2023 and was born on February 12, 1982, in New Orleans, Louisiana. The couple, who have an eight-year age difference, are expecting their first child.

Despite having three children from her previous marriage, Judy is excited to become a mother once again and start a new chapter in her life.

Judy is a successful American businesswoman, founder, and CEO of Kaleidoscope Hair Products. She opened her business, Kaleidoscope Hair Studio, in 2013 in New Orleans. 

She is also known for introducing a line of protective hairstyles designed by Da Brat. Furthermore, she has expanded her profession into real estate management, public speaking, mentorship, and philanthropy. 

As a single mother, she has successfully raised her children while managing her business and pursuing her passions. On the other hand, Da Brat is a renowned rapper who made history by being the first to have her debut album, Funkdafied, certified platinum in 1994. 

Despite being a successful rapper, she now focuses on starting a family with her wife. In an interview, she revealed that it had been quite a journey for the couple, and they are excited to welcome their first child.

The couple exchanged vows on February 2, 2022, and has since been sharing their love for each other on social media. They announced their pregnancy news on Instagram, with Judy sharing a photo of her growing baby bump.

The response to their announcement was overwhelming, with many fans and well-wishers congratulating the couple on their news. While the couple has not revealed the gender of their child, Da Brat revealed in an interview that Judy would like to name their child if they have a daughter. 

Da Brat also shared that their child would have all of her names, with Legacy Shawntae Dupart being the chosen name. She added that Shawntae is her first name, and Dupart is their baby’s last name, to carry on the legacy of their parents.

Judy and Da Brat’s journey towards starting a family may have been challenging, but they are excited to welcome their new bundle of joy. 

Despite the eight-year age difference between the couple, they share a deep love for each other, which has only grown stronger since they exchanged vows. They are an example of how love can conquer all and how starting a family at any age is beautiful.

When did Da Brat and Judy marry?

Sources report that Brat and Jessica Harris Dupart first met during a Miracle Drops hair product event in 2017. They quickly reconnected and built a strong friendship, eventually leading to a romantic relationship in 2019.

The couple celebrated their love by tying the knot in a beautiful wedding ceremony at the Horse Mansion in Georgia on February 22, 2022. Notable guests included Jermaine Dupri, Keith Sweat, and Brat’s sister Lisa Raye McCoy.

Their friends from Real Housewives of Atlanta were also in attendance, Kandi Burruss, Porsha Williams, and Eva Marcille. After their wedding, Brat expressed to PEOPLE how Jessica’s entire being made him want to spend the rest of his life with her.

Now, the couple eagerly awaited their first child’s arrival and joyfully shared their pregnancy news with the public.

At the young age of 15, Jessica Harris Dupart became a mother for the first time, and two years later, she welcomed her second child into the world.
