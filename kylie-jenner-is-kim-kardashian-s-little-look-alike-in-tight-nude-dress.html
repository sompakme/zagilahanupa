Kylie Jenner Is Kim Kardashian's Little Look-Alike in Tight Nude Dress

September 10, 2024
blog

In the nude. (Well, close!)

Kylie Jenner was spotted shopping for her new $2.7 million Calabasas mansion on Tuesday, June 30, and the raven-haired beauty worked her hourglass figure in a slinky nude dress—not unlike the kind her older half-sister Kim Kardashian would wear.

Staying close to boyfriend Tyga, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 17, was snapped leaving L.A.’s Z Gallerie wearing the body-conscious mini, accessorized with black sandal heels, Christian Dior shades, bangles, and a watch. She styled her long hair into loose waves, and rocked a lemon manicure.

Meanwhile, Tyga, 25, sported a distressed white T-shirt, teamed with textured jeans, white Converse sneakers, and a baseball cap. Rose gold baubles, including a bedazzled timepiece, completed his ensemble.

The day before, Jenner gave fans a more intimate peek at her moving process, sharing a snap of herself and her half-sister Khloe Kardashian standing on the paper-covered floors of her new home. The teen social media queen playfully captioned the shot, “Don’t you wish your move in crew looked like mine.”
