8 Singers Who Love Starring In Music Videos With Their Partners

July 19, 2024
blog

Many artists use their music videos to add deeper meaning to the lyrics in their songs. Some artists, like Michael Jackson, have made mini-movies for their videos. Other artists have used music videos to promote a positive message or raise awareness about a certain issue. Though Justin Bieber and Quavo’s song “Intentions” seems to be inspired by Justin’s wife Hailey Bieber, the music video shines a light on three low-income women of color in Los Angeles. Through her video for "Formation," Beyoncé speaks out against police brutality and showcases different aspects of Black culture in the South.

It is also not uncommon for artists to write songs about their relationships. However, even if a song is about their current relationship, an artist may still use a model to play their love interest in the music video. Sometimes, though, singers do ask their partners to star in their music videos. Continue reading to find out which singers have enjoyed starring in music videos with their partners again and again.

8 Adam Levine

Maroon 5's Adam Levine has been married to former Victoria's Secret model Behati Prinsloo since 2014. Though Adam never thought he would get married, the beautiful Behati changed his mind, and they now share two kids together. Behati has even used her modeling talents to star in Maroon 5's music videos. In the video for "Animals," Adam actually plays Behati's stalker. Behati has also starred in the videos for "Girls Like You," "Lost," and "Cold."

7 Justin Bieber

After rekindling their relationship in 2018, Justin and Hailey Bieber have already starred in several music videos together. She was in the music video for Justin's song with Dan + Shay, "10,000 Hours." Ariana Grande and Justin Bieber both featured their spouses in their video for "Stuck with U." Hailey also starred in Justin's second music video for "Anyone." Lastly, she made a cameo in the video for Drake's song "POPSTAR," alongside Justin.

6 John Legend

John Legend and Chrissy Teigen met on the set of John's music video for "Stereo," so, of course, Chrissy has continued to star in his videos. After their 2013 marriage, Chrissy starred in his videos for "All of Me," "Wild," "You & I," "Love Me Now," and "Preach." Chrissy even admitted that she once became extremely jealous when she visited the set of John's video for "Green Light," which starred "beautiful" women who were not her.

5 Jonas Brothers

Though they began their careers as a band of teenage heartthrobs, the Jonas Brothers are now a band of husbands. Kevin and his wife Danielle have been married since 2009. Nick was the second to get married. He married actress Priyanka Chopra in 2018. Finally, Joe married Game of Thrones actress Sophie Turner in 2019. The JoBros have shown off their gorgeous wives in their music videos for "Sucker" and "What A Man Gotta Do."

4 Thomas Rhett

Thomas Rhett first met his wife Lauren when they were in kindergarten. They became close friends in the sixth grade, dated briefly when they were fifteen, and got married at twenty-two. If his music is any indication, Thomas Rhett absolutely adores his wife, and he has even asked her to star in a few of his music videos. Lauren has been in his videos for "Die A Happy Man," "Life Changes," and "Look What God Gave Her."

3 A$AP Rocky

A$AP Rocky and Rihanna were good friends for years before becoming an official item and welcoming their first child together. They collaborated on a remix for Rihanna's song "Cockiness (Love It)," and Rihanna later starred in A$AP Rocky's video for "Fashion Killa." They reportedly started dating in 2020, and in 2022, Rihanna starred in A$AP's romantic video for his song "D.M.B."

2 Teyana Taylor

Teyana Taylor rapidly shot to fame when she starred in Kanye West's music video for "Fade" in 2016. Her husband, former NBA player Iman Shumpert, and their oldest daughter, Junie, also came along for the ride. This video was just a taste of what would come later on in their careers. Teyana and Iman also make their own music, and Iman starred in Teyana's music video for her song "Concrete." They also collaborate on each other's songs.

1 Pink

Pink and Carey Hart have been married since 2006 after Pink proposed to him at one of his motocross races. Though they have experienced rough patches throughout their relationship, they are still deeply in love with one another. Pink's "muse" has appeared in several of her videos, including the videos for "So What" and "Just Give Me A Reason." Pink reportedly had to do some extra nudging to get Carey to appear in some of these videos.
