William John “Bill” Bennett (born July 31, 1943) is an American conservative commentator, politician, and political theorist who is well-known for his work on the Fox News Channel. From 1985 until 1988, he was Secretary of Education under President Ronald Reagan. Additionally, he served as Director of the Office of National Drug Control Policy (ONDCP) under President George H. W. Bush. In 2000, he co-founded K12, a publicly listed online education business that is still in operation today.
