Background and Reasons for Layoffs

Discord, a popular communication platform, has experienced a significant surge in its workforce, with headcount growing fivefold since 2020. This rapid expansion was driven by the company’s success and the need to meet the demands of its growing user base. However, the exponential growth also brought challenges, such as decreased efficiency and operational bottlenecks.

The decision to implement layoffs at Discord is not indicative of a struggling business but rather a strategic move to address the inefficiencies that arose from rapid expansion. The company aims to sharpen its focus and improve collaboration by streamlining its operations and ensuring better agility within the organization.

“We grew too quickly and became less efficient in our day-to-day operations,” admitted Discord’s CEO, Jason Citron. “By reducing our workforce, we can better realign our resources to serve our users, enhance our business performance, and continue pursuing our long-term mission.”

Discord’s focus on efficiency is driven by the need to optimize its projects and maintain a high level of service for its users. By streamlining its workforce, the company can allocate resources effectively and ensure that its teams are working on the most critical initiatives.

Through this proactive approach to workforce reduction, Discord seeks to enhance its ability to deliver exceptional products, improve internal efficiency, and position itself for continued success in the highly competitive digital communication market.

Employee Impact and Support

Discord understands the impact of layoffs on its employees and is committed to providing comprehensive support during this transition period. The company has designed a package of resources and benefits to assist departing team members and ensure a smooth and positive experience as they pursue new opportunities.

Support for employees affected by the Discord layoffs includes:

  • Layoff Support: Departing employees will receive the equivalent of five months’ salary as severance. Additionally, they will receive an extra week of salary for each full year of service at Discord, acknowledging their contributions and dedication to the company.
  • Benefit Continuation: To ensure continuity of essential benefits, departing employees will continue to receive their benefits for a period of five months following the layoff.
  • Outplacement Services: Discord is providing three months of outplacement services to assist departing employees with job search and career transition support. These services aim to empower individuals to navigate the job market successfully and find new employment opportunities.
  • Equity Vesting: Employees with equity awards scheduled to vest in February 2024 will have their awards vest as planned, allowing them to benefit from the equity they have earned.
  • Mental Health Support: Recognizing the importance of mental well-being, Discord is granting departing employees continued access to Modern Health, a mental health support platform, through the end of 2024.

    • Discord expresses deep appreciation and gratitude for the contributions of the departing team members. The company values their dedication and optimism, and aims to provide the necessary resources and support to help them navigate their career transition successfully.
