8 Harry Styles's Career Began With One Direction

Harry Styles started making money as a singer after appearing on Britain's X Factor in 2010. He and four other contestants were put together to form the band One Direction, which would change their lives forever. Even though they didn't win the competition as solo acts, they became one of the biggest boy bands to come up in the last decade.

7 One Direction Endorsement Deals And Tours Made Harry Styles A Millionaire

The boy band made a huge chunk of their earnings through merchandise, concert tickets, and endorsements. Despite the 1D members having to split their paychecks five times between each other, Styles went home with a hefty sum after the group went on hiatus. The group's last tour, 2015's On The Road Again, made them $208 million in revenue, while their most successful tour, 2014's Where We Are, made $290 million.

Bringing in that kind of revenue made the group one of the most sought-after bands in the world. They worked with brands like Pepsi, Macy's, Colgate and Toyota. Per Forbes, 1D was the highest-earning European star in 2016, making $110 million from tour sales and endorsement payouts.

6 Harry Styles Has A Successful Solo Career

By the time Harry Styles was leaving One Direction to make it in music as a solo act, he was already worth $23 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth. When he decided to start his solo career, Styles signed a lucrative $80 million record deal with Columbia Records for three solo albums after Apple offered him $25 million for one album. His first single, Sign of The Times, was released in 2017 from his first album Harry Styles.
