10 Interesting Facts About Chris &amp; Liam Hemsworth's Relationship

Chris and Liam Hemsworth are two super attractive brothers who both know how to act. They have made headlines for movie roles they have snagged over the years. Liam Hemsworth's very public relationship, marriage, and divorce from Miley Cyrus also made major headlines. Chris and Liam Hemsworth are extremely popular and most likely always will be. There are a lot of interesting facts to uncover about their loving relationship.

They have a brotherly bond that is extremely strong and because of that, they always have each other‘s back. Sibling rivalry doesn’t necessarily exist between these two brothers since they always want what’s best for each other and are always willing to support each other in their personal lives and in their careers.

10 Chris & Liam Hemsworth Both Auditioned For The Role Of Thor

Chris Hemsworth is the brother who ended up landing the role of Thor in the MCU but once upon a time, Liam Hemsworth actually audition for the role as well. This just goes to show that sibling rivalry truly does not exist between these two brothers.

They were able to compete for the same part and still maintain the same strong relationship with one another despite the fact that one brother was chosen over the other. Liam Hemsworth was obviously very happy for his brother when Chris landed the role of Thor.

9 Chris & Liam Hemsworth Are Both More Well Known Than Their Brother Luke

Chris and Liam Hemsworth have another brother… Luke Hemsworth. Obviously, Chris and Liam are much more well known and their brother. Luke is also an actor who has appeared on shows like Westworld and Bikie Wars: Brothers in Arms.

All three of the Hemsworth brothers have the talent for acting in common but Chris and Liam are a bit more popular since the roles that they have landed are a lot bigger and more talked about in the media, like Hunger Games for example. Despite the fact that they are more well-known, they are still very close to their brother Luke.

8 Liam Admitted That Chris Is His Hero

Liam Hemsworth has admitted that he sees Chris Hemsworth as his hero. A lot of people in the world see Chris Hemsworth as their hero since he takes on the heroic role of Thor in the MCU. For Liam Hemsworth though, it is a more personal matter.

Chris is his actual big brother and he grew up with him, looking up to him his entire life. That being said, when Liam refers to Chris as his hero, he doesn’t mean it in a superhero way. He means it in a more emotional and affectionate way.

7 Liam Might Be Joining Chris In The MCU As Beta Ray Bill

Soon enough, Liam Hemsworth might be joining Chris Hemsworth in the MCU. Chris Hemsworth obviously already plays the role of Thor but Liam might be joining in the role of Beta Ray Bill. For comic book lovers, this character is an obvious one that should be added to the MCU.

With both of these brothers working together on marvel films, there would be nothing that could possibly hold them back. Seeing both Liam Hemsworth and Chris Hemsworth together in a marvel film would be an incredible thing.

6 As Kids, Liam Once Threw A Knife At Chris

As kids, Liam once threw a knife at Chris. They were fighting with each other but Liam admitted that he never really intended to hurt Chris when he threw the knife. Luckily, the other end of the knife opposite the blade is what hit Chris.

It would have been a messy situation if the blade inside actually hit Chris! These two brothers are very close these days and they do not get into fights or physical disputes the way that they did when they were children anymore. They are much more mature and are able to talk things through.

5 Liam Enjoys Being An Uncle To Chris' Kids

When describing his life as an uncle to Chris Hemsworth's kids, Liam Hemsworth said, "Just let them do whatever they want—candy, loads of candy, have candy. Beer, if you want one, I'll give you a beer."

He was obviously just joking about the beer part. Liam Hemsworth also described the time he let his nieces paint his fingernails and face for fun.

4 After Divorcing Miley Cyrus, Liam Spent Time In Byron Bay With Chris

Sometimes when people go through a breakup or emotional transition in life, the best thing they can do is go to a new environment in order to clear their heads. When Liam Hemsworth was going through his divorce with Miley Cyrus, he spent time in Byron beach, Australia with his older brother Chris Hemsworth.

It is important to spend time with people who are emotionally available when one is dealing with something as sad and difficult as a divorce. Chris was definitely there for Liam.

3 Chris Joked About Liam's Divorce From Miley Cyrus In Regards To Malibu

Miley Cyrus famously releases the song "Malibu" about her relationship with Liam Hemsworth. Chris Hemsworth jokingly talked about getting Liam out of Malibu in an interview which a lot of fans took as a dig against Miley Cyrus.

Even though they had a custody battle over their dogs, Miley and Liam are still cordial with each other and a lot of their devoted fans are holding out hope that they will reconcile one day since Miley Cyrus has recently become single again. Liam Hemsworth is not single but that doesn’t mean that at some point in the future, they may decide to try things out again.

2 Liam Gets Chris' Opinions About Scripts And Potential Roles

Liam Hemsworth spoke about his brother saying, "I've worked with a lot of people that have been in really great positions in the past 11 years, and Chris has a stronger work ethic than most. He’s so focused. I’m thankful that I have him and am able to use his resources and his knowledge. I call him all the time about scripts and get his opinion."

Chris Hemsworth obviously has no problems helping his younger brother Liam Hemsworth with some guidance about which roles to take on.

1 Chris & Liam Are Going To Film A Movie Together

Chris and Liam Hemsworth might very well be planning to film a movie together! Liam Hemsworth said, “We’re looking to do a film this year together, which is like a big action-comedy thing."

If these two brothers put out a movie together it would be incredible. Whether the movie is based in MCU or completely separate, anything that Liam Hemsworth and Chris Hemsworth plan to do together will most likely turn out in an epic way.
