Although Farrah Abraham is 32 years old now, Teen Mom fans still think of her as a young adult getting used to motherhood. What Did Farrah's Mom Debra Danielsen Do After Their Fight?

Farrah Abraham fought with other Teen Mom stars, but she has also argued with her mother Debra Danielsen on more than one occasion. It's impossible to look back on episodes of 16 & Pregnant and Teen Mom without remembering the moments when Farrah screamed at her mom... often for no apparent reason. Debra seemed to be on camera as much as Farrah was. Whether taking care of Sophia or trying to repair her bond with Farrah, Debra had a memorable presence.

Now that Farrah is no longer a Teen Mom cast member, fans want to know what Debra has been doing.

In the past few years, she has been exploring her love for rap. This seems to be something that means a lot to her. Of course, this announcement surprised many Teen Mom fans who had no idea that Debra wanted to be part of the music industry.

Debra Danielsen talked about rap in a 2017 interview with Reality Tea. She said that this was a good time in her life to make her passion a reality. She said, “I sort feel like a little teenager. Now I’ve got time to make music a bigger priority in my life.”

Debra also said that she loves Nicki Minaj and Drake. She told Reality Tea, "To me, rap music is talking about what’s real, what’s affecting people. I love it, I take it seriously and I think it’s ‘bring people together’ music.”

According to Starcasm, Debra put out a song called "Debz OG" in 2017. The music video can be viewed on her YouTube Channel. It doesn't appear that Debra made any money from this song. It's unclear if Debra hoped to make a living from music. However, in 2021, she gave several interviews and said that she was recording an album. While people can find her singles on Spotify, it doesn't appear that she ever put out a full-length album.

However, while her rap career didn't go anywhere, Debra is still showing her love for this type of music. She mostly seems to post on Instagram and TikTok, however. In April 2022, The Sun reported that she posted a video of herself rapping. She rapped the lyrics, "Chasing after capital like Texas. Flossin' - haters give me powers like I'm Austin. Check this Debra D. don't let them get close to me."

Debra's social media presence doesn't seem to be very popular, either.

In May 2023, Debra shared a TikTok that people didn't like at all. According to The Sun, Debra had a bodysuit on that many felt wasn't completely appropriate. In the video, she said, "Today, I wish you love, joy, and happiness. Remember to have empathy, and then you will be like the stars shining brightly in the sky, and your dreams will take you very high."

Many people posted on the video and said that they were unhappy with it. The Sun reported that Debra didn't seem bothered by that at all and wrote "Positive vibes."

What Is Farrah Abraham's Mom Debra Danielson Doing Now?

Both Farrah Abraham and her mom Debra Danielsen continue to cause controversy with their social media posts. Debra seems to be happy, though, and people's opinions don't appear to phase her in the least.

As for what Debra is up to in 2023, she might not be spending that much time with her family. Over the past few years, several news stories have reported that Debra and Farrah are still angry with each other.

The Sun reported that in 2021, Debra was interviewed on Have a Seat with Chris Hansen. She shared her desire to be part of Farrah and Sophia's lives. She said, “We’re not speaking today, so that’s very sad, and I miss my granddaughter..." She continued, "I pray to God that my daughter is happy and healthy, and I do want [our] relationship to be happy and healthy... I want my Sophia to know that she is still the sparkle in my eye and I will always love her and I miss her.”

People curious about Debra's personal life might have heard rumors that her marriage hasn't been going well. According to People, Debra and Dr. David Merz got married in 2017. Despite the drama between Farrah and her mom, Farrah was still there.

In April 2021, Debra told The Sun that she and David were fine. She said, "We are doing awesome. The album is about my dating/fiancé journey. My husband is the perfect match for me to heal and realize how dysfunctional these others were."

As for what else Farrah Abraham's mom Debra Danielsen has been up to, she has also been talking publicly about her daughter. She still appears on reality TV from time to time as well.

In March 2022, Debra talked about the TV show Teen Mom OG Family Reunion in an interview with The Ashley's Reality Roundup. She said that she didn't like how Farrah acted on the TV series.

Debra explained, “If people can’t love themselves, it’s very scary. On the inside that’s causing so much uncertainty. She doesn’t have peace."

What's next for Debra Danielsen? She seems to be at peace with her marriage and her rap career. While it's unclear if she and Farrah Abraham will ever make up or find a way to tolerate each other, it seems likely that Debra will keep trying to reach her daughter.
