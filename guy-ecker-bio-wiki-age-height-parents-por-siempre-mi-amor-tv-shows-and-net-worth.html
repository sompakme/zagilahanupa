Guy Ecker Biography

Guy Ecker was born on February 9, 1959, as Guy Frederick Ecker, is a Brazilian American actor better known for his work in telenovelas. He became a household name in Latin America for his portrayal of Sebastián Vallejo in the Colombian telenovela Café, con aroma de mujer.

In 1998, Ecker moved to Mexico and won his first leading role in a Mexican telenovela, playing opposite Kate del Castillo in the telenovela La Mentira, which also became a huge success. Additionally, American viewers may be familiar with him through his work on the television show Las Vegas as Detective Luis Perez.
