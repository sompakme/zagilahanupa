Nolan’s known for his expertise in developing movies using complex theories and advanced scientific plots. Tenet is no exception as it is an action sci-fi based on a mission for the survival of the entire world. The movie’s plot is confusing, and however, it emphasizes the destruction caused when manipulated objects used as weapons fall into the wrong hands. A CIA agent named the Protagonist must save the world as few things unfold in the future. The film boasts an incredible cast, including John David Washington, Elizabeth Debicki, and Robert Pattinson, and the Warner Bros studios produced the movie.

A film with a gigantic budget, Tenet, has a classic Nolan edge. The plot's standard characteristics, such as time-bending, out-of-order chronology, and crime drama, keep the audience awe-struck.

An Overview Of The Plot

John plays the role of an unnamed CIA agent known as The Protagonist who participated in an undercover operation at a Kyiv Opera House. He, employed by an organization named Tenet, whose mission is to ensure the human race's survival. As on The Movie Culture, a masked soldier with a distinctive trinket helps him. The soldier appears to fire a bullet through a hostile gunman. The Russian mercenaries capture The Protagonist after he rescued an exposed spy and seized a strange artifact. John was tortured in the film before he consumed a cyanide pill. The agent later learns that the pill was fake, his team got killed, and the artifact was lost.

The Plot’s Confusion Among The Actors

There was some confusion among actors as they couldn't crack the plot well. John David Washington, who played the main character in the movie, admitted that he always had questions for Christopher while shooting the film. Robert Pattinson, too, had a hard time understanding the plot, which left him disoriented. Even after spending months reading through his lines and working on the set, he could not determine what was going on in the film reported by Cheat Sheet. Dimple Kapadia, an Indian actress who played the role of a spy agent in the movie, also reported that she would have to watch the film at least three more times before she could understand anything.

The Budget And Box Office Numbers

Screenrant reports that the production budget of Tenet is around $200-225 million. It is one of the most expensive films in Nolan's career. The budget on Tenet is higher than that of Christopher’s previous films like The Dark Knight($185 million), Inception($160 million), and Interstellar($165 million). It is more like The Dark Knight Rises, which had a budget of around $250 million. Going by the general rule, Tenet would have to make at least $400 million to recoup the cost of production.

Reports suggest that the film needs to earn $800 million to profit. The film was originally scheduled to premiere in July but got delayed due to the COVID-19 Pandemic. As Nolan’s movie is finally released, it is one of the first major films to come out in theaters since multiplexes reopened after months. International box-office relies on the film to be a much-needed boost for business after being walloped during the crisis. So far, it has made $362.92 million in the worldwide box office and $50 million in domestic video sales.

Current Net Worth Of The Cast 

Observer says that Christopher Nolan stands to make a ton of money if Tenet breaks out as the blockbuster Warner Bros hopes it can be. Nolan is expected to receive 20 percent of movie's gross. This means that Nolan will get a percentage of the box office revenue beginning with the film's release rather than when the movie returns a profit. Nolan has made such deals with WB, but it is still a privilege reserved for a few filmmakers.

The net worth of different actors and crew is a factor in determining the cost of production. Christopher Nolan has a net worth of $180 million. At present, the lead actors John David Washington, Pattinson, and Elizabeth boast a net worth of $8 million, $100 million, $1.9 million, respectively. The supporting cast includes Dimple Kapadia, Michael Caine, and Kenneth Branagh, who have a net worth of $10 million, $80 million, and $60 million, respectively.

Overall, Tenet is a big-budget movie by Christopher Nolan with Warner Bros Production. It is one of Nolan's most expensive films and expects to make at least $400 million at the box office. Nolan stands to gain a massive amount of money if the film becomes a blockbuster, as WB hopes it would be.
