The Cast Of Wednesday, Ranked By Net Worth

July 19, 2024
blog

Netflix’s Wednesday series has taken the world by storm. The show which is based on The Addams Family focuses on the iconic character that is Wednesday Addams. The series dropped on Netflix on November 23, and viewers have been hooked ever since with Wednesday setting and breaking its own record for hours watched in its first two weeks. It became the first English-language series to stream over 400 million hours in one week. As of writing, Wednesday sits at No. 2 in the U.S after it was dethroned by Netflix’s new thriller/drama series The Recruit which stars Noah Centineo with Katharine King.

The series introduces us to some new faces and introduces some veterans to an entirely new demographic. Stars like Christina Ricci have been in Hollywood for decades; she played Wednesday Addams in the ‘90s Addams Family movies. Catherine Zeta-Jones and Luis Guzman who portray Morticia and Gomez Addams, have also been in the business for a while and have racked up quite impressive resumes. While the cast of Wednesday is new to many, they’d made a name for themselves before starring in the series.

We ranked the cast of Wednesday based on the net worth the actors have amassed over the years. Keep reading to see which of the stars of the Netflix series is the richest, and how they made their fortune.

8 How Much Is Hunter Doohan’s Net Worth?

According to Popular Net Worth, Hunter Doohan is reportedly worth $400,000, most of which he earned from acting in Your Honor and other projects.

As one of the breakout stars of Wednesday, the actor’s fortune is expected to increase in tandem with his increasing popularity on social media. Hunter has racked up millions of new followers on Instagram since the series debuted on Netflix.

7 Jamie McShane Has Been Acting For A While, But How Rich Is He?

Jamie McShane has been acting since the ‘90s so it’s quite surprising the veteran’s net worth is just about $1.2 million as reported by Popular Net Worth.

McShane who plays Sheriff Donovan Galpin in Wednesday has played everything including roles in Star Trek: Picard (2020), CSI: Vegas (2021), and The Avengers (2012).

6 Jenna Ortega’s Net Worth Is On The Rise

Jenna Ortega plays the titular role of Wednesday Addams in series, but she’s earned most of her $3 million net worth as reported by Celebrity Net Worth, from her time at Disney and other roles including playing Tara Carpenter in Scream (2022), and Ellie Alves in You (2019).

Her role on Wednesday has made Jenna Ortega an even bigger name; the actress’ net worth is expected to rise with her growing fame.

5 How Rich Is Gwendoline Christie ?

Gwendoline Christie played Principal Larissa Weems in the series but before that she played Brienne of Tarth on Game of Thrones from 2012 to the end of the show in 2019.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Christie has accumulated an estimated net worth of $4 million from her role on Game of Thrones and others including in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 2 (2015), The Sandman (2022), and several Star Wars projects.

4 How Much Is Percy Hynes White Worth?

Besides playing Xavier Thorpe on Wednesday, Percy Hynes White is probably known for portraying Andy Strucker on Fox’s The Gifted (2017-2019).

Percy Hynes White has a net worth of around $9 million according to Popular Net Worth, which the actor made from these and many other roles including his starring in Pretty Hard Cases (2021-2022), and the TV movie A Killer In My Home (2020).

3 Luis Guzman’s Net Worth Is Quite Surprising

Fans of The Addams Family have been split on Luis Guzman’s being cast as Gomez Addams in the Netflix series. Celebrity Net Worth reports that the actor has an estimated net worth of just $13 million even though he has a long resume that dates back to 1977.

Guzman has earned his fortune through roles in Oz (1998-200), A Series of Unfortunate Events (2004), and Shameless (2019).

2 How Much Is Christina Ricci Worth After Three Decades In The Business?

Christina Ricci is one of the biggest names on this list, with credits to her name including her portrayal of Wednesday Addams in The Addams Family from the ‘90s.

According to Popular Net Worth, the veteran has accumulated an estimated net worth of $22 million from starring in Penelope (2006), Here After (2020), The Matrix Resurrections (2021) and many other projects.

1 This Is How Catherine Zeta-Jones Makes Her Massive Net Worth?

Catherine Zeta-Jones who plays Morticia Addams is the richest cast member on Wednesday, with an estimated net worth of $150 million as per Celebrity Net Worth.

In spite of her high net worth, Catherine Zeta-Jones is not the most experienced actor in the series, as most of her net worth comes from her various endorsements, including becoming a global ambassador for Elizabeth Arden in 2002 and signing a $10 million-dollar deal with T-Mobile in the same year. In 2017, Catherine Zeta-Jones also launched her own line of home decor items.
