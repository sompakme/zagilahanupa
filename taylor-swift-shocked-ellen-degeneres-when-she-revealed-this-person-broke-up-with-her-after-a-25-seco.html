What Happened Between Ellen DeGenerse And Taylor Swift On The 'Ellen Show'?

Looking back, Taylor Swift agrees that she did take things a little too far during the particular moment on the 'Ellen Show', calling out her then-boyfriend Joe Jonas.

Taylor Swift Went Into Details About Her Breakup From Joe Jonas

It all went completely south when Ellen made mention of Joe Jonas. Not only did Swift reveal the breakup, but she also went in depth, discussing exactly how they broke up.

"You know what, it’s like, when I find that person that is right for me, and he’ll be wonderful, and when I look at that person, I’m not even gonna be able to remember the boy who broke up with me over the phone in 25 seconds when I was 18,” Swift continued. “I looked at the call log, it was like 27 seconds.
