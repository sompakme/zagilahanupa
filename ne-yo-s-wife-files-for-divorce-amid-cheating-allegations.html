Famed performer Ne-Yo, who quietly made music moves in recent years, has been in the headlines after his wife accused him of cheating during their entire relationship.Ne-Yo (real name Shaffer Chimere Smith) allegedly cheated for eight years, though his wife Crystal Renay Smith, who spilled the details, seemingly deleted her Instagram post detailing the drama.At the time of Smith's post, it was unclear whether she was immediately filing for divorce. The deletion of her post also caused speculation; would she go back to Ne-Yo after calling him a narcissist and saying that she was choosing herself over her allegedly unfaithful husband?

Crystal Renay Smith Filed For Divorce From Ne-Yo

Though Crystal didn't specify whether she had actually left Ne-Yo, or if she would be filing for divorce, on social media, she did take legal action.

Per the Los Angeles Times, Ne-Yo's wife filed for divorce and stated that the former couple had separated in late July 2022. The filing stated that the marriage was "irrevocably broken."

Ne-Yo Asked For Privacy Amid The Divorce Filing
