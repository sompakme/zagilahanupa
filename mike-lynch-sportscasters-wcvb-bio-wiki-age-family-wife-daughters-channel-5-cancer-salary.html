Mike Lynch WCVB Channel 5

Mike currently works at WCVB Channel 5 as the Senior Sports Anchor and Director since 1982. He also hosts the ‘SportsCenter 5 OT’ and the “High Five” program. He has also worked for New England Patriots as the Preseason Sideline Reporter from 1995 to 2008.

He was profiled as the “Sportscaster of the years” in 2012 by the National Sportscasters and Sportswriters Association in 1985-1991, 1999, 2003, and 2006-2012 for a total of 16 times. SportsCenter 5 won the United Press International Award for “Best Sports Reporting” in 1987 in the Country.

The Prior to joining WCVB, he worked at WMRE Radio in Boston as a Sports director as well as for WITS. He also as a broadcast commentator during Patriots pre-season games for “Patriots All Access”. He has also done play-by-play coverage for the Boston Celtics, New England Patriots, and college football. For eleven years, he hosted the Boston College weekly football coach’s show “Eagles Preview” on Channel 5.
