Heres What Sofia Vergara Is Asking For In Her Million-Dollar Divorce

August 15, 2024
blog

Sofia Vergara has officially responded to Joe Manganiello’s request for divorce, and the actress is fighting to ensure their prenup is upheld.

According to court documents obtained by PEOPLE Magazine, Sofia put the same reason as Joe for their split – “irreconcilable differences.” In her filing, she asked that their estate be divided according to the terms of their prenup, which includes all jewelry, artwork, and “other personal effects” remain in her possession.

Sofia also wants to ensure her earnings from before and after their marriage are protected.

It was previously reported that the former couple signed a $100 million, iron-clad prenup prior to tying the knot, which in theory should make the split relatively easily.

While Joe is worth a reported $40 million, Sofia has a net worth of $180 million. In addition to her role on Modern Family, Sofia has amassed a large portion of her wealth from her entertainment company, Latin World Entertainment.

Joe and Sofia released a joint statement in mid-July announcing their intention to divorce after 7 years of marriage. "We have made the difficult decision to divorce,” it read. “As two people that love and care for one another very much, we politely ask for respect for our privacy at this time as we navigate this new phase of our lives.”

Reports have said Joe and Sofia broke up because of his desire to have children. The actress has a 31-year old son from a previous relationship, and apparently doesn’t want to have any more children now that she’s 51-years old.

Though Joe and Sofia’s split appears to be amicable, previous reports say there was one thing the pair was disagreeing on – custody of their 10-year old dog, Bubbles, which they adopted together two years ago.

“They still have a few issues to figure out, like custody of their dog, Bubbles ,” a source told Us Weekly . “But Sofia hopes they can move forward in a calm, fair fashion.”

However, shortly after it was reported that Sofia agreed to give custody over to her estranged husband.

For now, Joe and Sofia’s divorce remains ongoing.
