Christmas is that time of the year when we love the spirit of giving, of hope and love. Celebrities love to spend Christmas with their families and share gifts and presents just like everyone else. As the Christmas season is also time for vacations, celebrities get the time off to spend it well.

Preparations for the holiday start early and themes are developed every year. Some celebrities do go crazy for Christmas sometimes and have a blast preparing for the season. We would like to share what ten celebrities do for their Christmas celebrations. Check out this list and you could probably add some new traditions to yours as well.

Kelly Clarkson

Kelly Clarkson is our favorite American Idol is one who loves the Christmas season a lot. Her festival season special on NBC, Kelly Clarkson’s Cautionary Christmas Music Tale, is a wonderful way in which she shows her love for the holiday. This lover of Christmas has a tradition that she does every year for the celebration. She and her family create some of the best Christmas cards ever.

Every year, Kelly Clarkson and her family make great Christmas cards that bring the love around. Her card with her new family is really hilarious to see. She really captures the spirit of Christmas in a fun way.

The Kardashians

A big family is always a reason to celebrate Christmas in a grand way. The Kardashians and Jenners are truly crazy about Christmas and have special ways in which to celebrate the holiday season. Kylie Jenner and Kim Kardashian are big on Christmas trees and they love to decorate these massive trees with all things sparkling. Khloe Kardashian, on the other hand, loves to put up pictures of her with her nieces and nephews.

Most of them are her snuggling with them, all dressed in their favorite pajamas of the season. The Kardashian-Jenner clan is also known for its fantastic Christmas cards that they send out to all family and friends.

Mariah Carey

This Christmas queen is known for some of her Christmas music with hits like “All I Want for Christmas Is You” and others. She has two full-length Christmas albums that are best-sellers and loved by all. She also has a very special Hallmark Christmas movie titled, A Christmas Melody.

When Mariah is not singing and dishing out Christmas music, she is busy preparing for the holiday season with full gusto. Her Instagram account is full of fun-filled pictures of all her antics and holiday decorations. She looks lovely in red and this is one prominent color she seems to wear during the holiday season.

Neil Patrick Harris

We all love Neil Patrick Harris and his family. So, anything that he does for Christmas is adored by his fans too. Neil goes all out for Christmas and embraces the festive season with both arms. His Christmas tree is decorated with the most unique tree ornaments and are adorable to look at. Apart from the gorgeous Christmas tree decorations, Neil also has a great gifting list for his beautiful kids.

From dollhouses to life-sized rockets, they get it all. This is really enchanting, to say the least. Neil also indulges in the Christmas movie celebrations by starring in great ones like The Christmas Wish and The Christmas Blessing.

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon is another Christmas-obsessed celebrity who loves to come up with new creations every year. She has also starred in the movie Four Christmases. With her creation “Wreath Witherspoons,” she brought about the great idea of Christmas wreaths decorated with love.

Her recent business launch is Draper James, which is a lifestyle company, dealing in many holiday sweaters among other gorgeous things. Check out Reese’s Instagram page and you will find cool party ideas, letters to Santa and other great tips for celebrating the Christmas season with gusto and fun. Reese Witherspoon sure knows how to party during Christmas!

Katy Perry

Katy Perry can roar all about Christmas and her love for this fabulous holiday season. We know she loves to sing and singing for Christmas is what most musicians love to do. This is one reason to be happy and joyful. Katy urges her fans to jump for joy, wear uggs every day and be in their active-wear at night. Katy Perry wants her fans to be cool and just enjoy this holiday season.

You will find many of Katy’s pictures for the holidays are those where she is jumping in front of malls, just being a silly girl. This is how Katy Perry loves to spend her Christmas season – full of love, fun, and joy.

Miley Cyrus

Another musician who loves the Christmas season is Miley Cyrus. This famous star loves her canine companions too and always sees to it that they are treated well during the holidays. Along with her family, Miley gifts her dogs the best presents for Christmas. Most of her holiday pics are with her dogs – hugging, cuddling and pampering them a whole lot.

Miley loves to dress up in holiday gear with starry prints and bright colored designs. With many Instagram pictures that she posts during the holidays, Miley Cyrus gives you a glimpse into her life around this beautiful, blessed season.

Drew Barrymore

Drew Barrymore is a much-loved actress who truly embodies the Christmas spirit. You will find her obsessed with Christmas and Christmas trees. She loves to decorate her trees and then loves to click selfies! Check out all her tree selfies taken from all directions and you will know how crazy she is about the Christmas season.

What we adore about her selfies is that they are not practiced poses. They are just simple, spur-of-the-moment clicks without the makeup and fuss taken for that perfect picture. Drew just loves the simplicity of the season and takes in the charm and spirit of the holidays.

Lucy Hale

This star of the hit series The Pretty Little Liars is also someone who is obsessed with Christmas. She loves to spend the holiday season with her family. Baking and eating are two of the best things that this holiday time gives her. She loves to bake cookies with her two cute kids. Baking, decorating and then munching on these delectable cookies are how Lucy spends her Christmas time.

You will find most of her pictures filled with decorations and food that she has cooked at home. Lucy Hale makes the Christmas season a lovely time that her kids will enjoy and cherish forever.

Kristin Cavallari

Another star who loves the Christmas spirit is Kristin Cavallari, who is a former reality star. Though Christmas was always a special occasion for her, it is now even more meaningful and fun. Now that she is a mother of three kids, she loves to spend the time with her family. She enjoys adding new traditions to the Christmas season so that her kids have something to look forward to.

Elf on the Shelf is a new thing that Kristin started with her kids. She loves the holidays and all the fun elements that are associated with it. Decorating, cooking, with her kids is what makes the season very precious.
