Three gorgeous women are more than what you expect. Natalie (Cameron Diaz), Dylan (Drew Barrymore) and Alex (Lucy Liu) are highly trained private investigators who work for Charlie. They can handle everything from state of the art vehicles and gadgets. The trio can even take on their biggest enemies whether flying in the air, scuba diving in the seas or using their martial arts on land.

The film Charlie's Angels premiered in 2,000 based on the 1976 television series of the same name. Forget femme fatale or saving the princess, Charlie's Angels can take care of themselves and are the ones doing the saving. It's been twenty years since the film's debut, let's take a look on where the cast is now.

Crispin Glover (Thin Man)

Crispin Glover played one of the side villains in the film. He was contracted to kill the Angels during their mission to stop Eric Knox and Redstar. Glover's character was a bit creepy with oily black hair, pale complexion, and a three-piece suit. He also had an obsession with women's hair. You might remember Glover for playing George McFly in Back to the Future or The Knave of Hearts in Alice in Wonderland. 

Since the film, Glover has taken on many projects including continuing to write books. His latest book was in 2016 titled Round My House. In 2018, he was a part of the films The Con is On and We Have Always Lived in a Castle. In American Gods, he played Mr.World for nine episodes and was part of the television film Seed of Terror. He is also in 2019 film Lucky Day.

Tom Green (Chad)

Tom Green played a minor character in Charlie's Angels, but one to remember fondly. He played Chad, one of Dylan's former love interests. He's eccentric, lives on a boat and is madly in love with Dylan. Tom Green was also briefly married to Drew Barrymore.

Green is mostly known for hosting television shows, The New Tom Green Show in 2003 and Tom Green Live from 2013 to 2014. In 2013, he started an audio podcast titled Tom Green Radio. Now, he performs stand-up comedy and had his performance air on Showtime in 2011.

Tim Curry (Roger Corwin)

Roger Corwin is the supposed kidnapper of Eric Knox and owner of software company Redstar in the film. He is played by actor Tim Curry. Curry is notably known for playing the original Pennywise in the 1990 horror mini-series IT. He was also Dr. Frank-N-Furter in the horror picture show The Rocky Horror Picture Show.

Due to a stroke that caused Curry to use a wheelchair, he mainly does voice acting now. He has lent his voice to films Axel the Biggest Hero, Ribbit and Long Drive Home. He also did voice acting Star Wars: The Clone Wars. Curry has a list of audiobooks including Dracula and A Christmas Carol. 

Kelly Lynch (Vivian Wood)

In Charlie's Angels, Kelly Lynch plays Vivian Wood who is Eric Knox's right hand and assistant. It later comes out that she is also part of Knox's master plan. Lynch has played many leading roles including alongside Patrick Swayze in Road House. She also played Laurel Coope in 90210 from 2010 to 2011.

Lynch is still acting having taken part in the 2015 films, The Frontier and Kepler's Dream. In 2012 and 2013 she played the recurring role of Meg Bannock in Magic City. The crime drama seroes Mr.Mercedes (2017), Lynch played the main role of Deborah Hartsfield.

Sam Rockwell (Eric Knox)

Sam Rockwell continues has continued his acting repertoire after playing Eric Knox in Charlie's Angels. Eric Knox is a sweet, smart guy who charmed the Angels, but in reality, he is more than meets the eye.

Rockwell continued to play roles in various films including Iron Man 2, Conviction and Mr.Right. In 2017, he won an Oscar for Best Supporting Actor in the film Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri. In 2018, he portrayed George W.Bush in the comedy-drama Vice. He played Captain Klenzendorf in the film Jojo Rabbit. Rockwell also played Bob Fosse in the limited series Fosse/Verdon. 

Luke Wilson (Peter Kominsky)

Peter Kominsky plays a supporting role as Natalie's love interest in Charlie's Angels. He is played by actor Luke Wilson. Shortly after the film, he starred opposite Reese Witherspoon in Legally Blonde. Wilson is recognized for playing roles in the films My Super Ex-Girlfriend, Death at a Funeral and The Ridiculous 6. In 2011 until 2013, he starred in the HBO television series Enlightened.

In 2019, he played Albuquerque in the comedy film Zombieland: Double Tap. It was also announced in 2019, Wilson would play Pat Dugan/S.T.R.I.P.E in the DC Universe series Stargirl. 

Bill Murray (John Bosley)

Bill Murray plays lovable John Bosley in the film. Bosley is the secretary/assistant to the Angels. Bosely is a type of middleman between Charlie and the Angels. Murray is a famous actor in Hollywood mostly recognized for Saturday Night Live and playing Venkman in the Ghostbuster films.

Since Charlie's Angels, Murray has continued his work as ana actor. He was Vincent MacKenna in St.Vincent (2014), Baloo in The Jungle Book (2016) and Martin Heiss in Ghostbusters (2016). In 2019, he played roles in the films The Dead Don't Die and Zombieland: Double Tap. He reprises his role as Venkman in the upcoming Ghostbusters: Afterlife. 

Lucy Liu (Alex Munday)

Lucy Liu plays one-third of the Charlie's Angels trio. Alex is highly skilled in combat, explosives, bomb assembly and diffusion and highly intelligent. She was a government aerospace engineer for NASA. Alex is also a trained fencer and horse rider.

Lucy Liu has since played many recognized roles including O-Ren Ishii in Kill Bill and Shanghai Noon with Jackie Chan and Owen Wilson. From 2012 until 2019, Liu played Joan Watson in the television series Elementary. She is also the lead role in Why Women Kill as Simone. Liu has also done voice acting for the Kung Fu Panda films as Master Viper. In 2018 and 2019, she directed episodes for Luke Cage, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and Why Women Kill. Lucy Liu is also an artist with her latest exhibit on display at the National Museum of Singapore in 2019.

Cameron Diaz (Natalie Cook)

Cameron Diaz played the blonde and tall bombshell, Natalie Cook. Natalie can be described at the more freeform of the trio. She is highly knowledgeable in birds and bird feces. She is starry-eyed and goofy, but a pro behind the wheel. Natalie is skilled in road racing, helicopter piloting and motocross. Diaz, since playing the role, has had many iconic moments in film.

Diaz voiced Fiona in the Shrek films. She was in the film In Her Shoes(2005) and the psychological thriller film The Box (2009). She co-wrote the book The Body Book: Feed, Move, Understand and Love Your Amazing Body. In the 2014 film Sex Tape, she starred alongside Jason Segal. She married Good Charlotte musician, Benji Madden in 2015. In 2017, after appearing in the film adaptation of Annie, Diaz announced her retirement from acting. She has since invested in health and biotech startups.

Drew Barrymore (Dylan Sanders)

Dylan Sanders is played by actor Drew Barrymore. Dylan can be described as the rambunctious and rebellious type. She is also the tomboy of the trio. Although she is not necessarily skilled in a specific area, she is more than capable to fight her way out of trouble. Drew Barrymore has since been a part of many films including Fever Pitch, Confessions of a Dangerous Mind and played Lauren Reynolds in Blended.

She launched a range of cosmetics in 2013 under the name Flower and later released a memoir titled Wildflower.  In 2017 until 2019, she played Sheila Hammond in the recently cancelled Netflix series Santa Clarita Diet. Barrymore is also producer having taken part in He's Just Not That Into You, Whip It, the 2011 television update of Charlie's Angels, How to be Single, and the latest Charlie's Angels reboot.
