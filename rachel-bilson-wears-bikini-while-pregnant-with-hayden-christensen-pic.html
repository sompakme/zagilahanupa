Bilson’s bumpin’ bikini bod! Now that the cat’s out of the bag, Rachel Bilson is letting her growing baby bump hang out for the world to see. Bilson wore yet another two-piece, leaving her blossoming bump out as her doting boyfriend stayed close by.
