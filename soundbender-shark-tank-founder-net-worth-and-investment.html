About SoundBender and Its Benefits

SoundBender is a compact and cost-effective solution to enhance the audio quality of iPads. It attaches magnetically to the device’s speaker, redirecting the sound towards the user. The product is power-free, requiring no external power source or batteries. SoundBender is compatible with most iPad models and can be used with iPad covers. It amplifies volume, improves sound quality, and is ideal for activities like watching videos, playing games, or using Facetime.
