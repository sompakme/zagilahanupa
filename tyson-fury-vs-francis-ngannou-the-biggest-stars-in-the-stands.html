On October 28th, two of the greatest fighters went head-to-head in a collision to find out who was the baddest man on the planet.

Tyson Fury, who is the WBC heavyweight champion (of boxing), and Francis Ngannu, who is the former UFC Heavyweight champion of the world, put on a show that drew all kinds of stars from around the world to watch an epic battle in the grand arena of Saudi Arabia.

After a close fight, Tyson Fury got the better of Francis Ngannu and won a split decision.

While it was a big crossover fight to find out who was the superior, more important was the truckloads of money both expected to receive.

Tyson Fury claimed Francis Ngannu would net a respectable $10 million from this fight; Dereck Cherosa claimed Tyson Fury would walk away with a massive $50 million in his deal with Saudi Arabia.

Though the fight was a blockbuster event in and of itself, what captured people’s attention most was the flood of stars and celebrities who came to watch it.

If you were too focused on the fight, here are some of the biggest names spotted at the event.

Net Worth: Approximately $500 Million

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the best football players of all time and the most followed person on Instagram, with more than 600 million fans.

Ronaldo was sitting in the front row with his partner, Georgina Rodriguez.

A five-time Ballon d'Or winner (with a staggering net worth of $500 million), CR7 is one of the wealthiest sportsmen in the world.

His presence at the event was icing on the cake, bringing more intrigue to the clash of the two titans of the ring.

Conor McGregor

Net Worth: Approximately $200 Million

If you know about the UFC, you surely know the name Conor McGregor; Conor is not just a fighter, but the guy who made the sport (UFC) the major attraction it is today.

The amount of success he achieved in the UFC and the revenue he generated for the business and himself is unmatchable.

He is the wealthiest fighter in the UFC, with a massive $200 million net worth.

McGregor's "Notorious" presence added interest to the bout, for fans of the sport in particular.

Eminem

Net Worth: Approximately $250 Million

Marshall Mathers is better known by his stage name Eminem.

He is one of the greatest rappers of all time, and is credited with introducing hip-hop music to middle America.

Eminem's songs accumulate billions of streams, and his talent has earned him a vast fortune, resulting in a colossal net worth of $250 million.

Kanye West

Net Worth: Approximately $400 Million

One of the most popular rappers and songwriters (who has won 24 Grammy Awards), Kanye West is a big name in the music industry with millions of followers.

Along with Eminem, he was at the event enjoying the action in the ring, and presumably mingling with his fellow A-listers.

With all the success and fame he has from recording and producing, it is no wonder that Kanye West retains an impressive net worth of $400 million.

Ronaldo Nazario

Net Worth: Approximately $160 Million

Ronaldo Nazario is one of the best player the fertile football soil of Brazil ever produced.

In 2002, he led Brazil all the way to World Cup glory - their record fifth championship crown.

The two-time Ballon d'Or winner was a distinguished athlete who had a unique style and elegance in his play.

With the success he had on the football pitch, Nazario accumulated a net worth of around $160 million.

Larry Holmes

Net Worth: Approximately $18 Million

Larry Holmes is a professional American heavyweight boxer who reigned as a champion for many years.

Regarded as one of the best in the history of boxing, Larry Holmes was the fighter who beat the great Ali in 1980.

The boxer has an impressive record of 69 wins and six losses in his time in the ring.

Across an illustrious career, Holmes accumulated a massive wealth of $18 million – and was included in the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 2008.

Mike Tyson

Current Net Worth: Approximately $10 To $15 Million

One of the most iconic and most feared heavyweight boxers who has ever lived, Mike Tyson was never the type to take fights "deep," and instead just finished off his opponents in early rounds.

Tyson was in attendance for this mega-fight, but not just as a spectator.

Iron Mike was the person who trained Ngannu for his fight against Fury and, as such, was in Ngannu's corner for this star-studded showdown.

Mike Tyson still holds the record of being the youngest heavyweight champion in the world, earned when he was just 20 years old.

In the course of his legendary boxing career, Tyson was able to accumulate a fortune of $430 million – later losing the majority of his money.

The Undertaker

Net Worth: Approximately $20 Million

Mark Wiliam Claway - known by the ring name “Undertaker” - is a retired professional wrestler who was a WWE superstar and has millions of fans around the world.

The Undertaker's inimitable presence and general unique style made him an icon in the world of wrestling. Often called “The Dead Man,” he put on a show of which very could rival.

His ability to routinely draw crowds of millions to the WWE helped him earn a fierce net worth estimated at around $20 million.

Amir Khan

Net Worth: Approximately $40 Million

Net Worth: Approximately $30 Million

Sadio Mane is a star Senegalese footballer who exemplifies the perfect balance of humility and pure talent.
