AEW Women’s Tournament Announced

The No. 1 contender for the All Elite Wrestling Women’s Championship will be crowned in an upcoming tournament. AEW announced the AEW Women’s World Championship Eliminator Tournament tonight. Details were limited, but the graphic shows a 16-woman tournament and has the United States and Japanese flags.

Tag Team Battle Royal Added To AEW Beach Break

AEW Beach Break takes place in just two weeks and now it has a high stakes tag team battle royal. The winner of the match will get a tag title shot at AEW Revolution, the promotion’s next pay-per-view. The tag belts are currently held by the Young Bucks.
